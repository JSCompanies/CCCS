About CCCS

The Cleveland County Choral Society is North Carolina's most distinguished community choir, dedicated to enriching lives through the transformative power of choral music. Founded with a vision to elevate the cultural landscape of Shelby, CCCS stands as a beacon of artistic excellence.

Our mission extends beyond performances—we foster community connection, support local artists, and provide scholarships to emerging musicians. With a repertoire spanning classical masterworks, contemporary compositions, and spiritual arrangements, we captivate audiences across the region.
