About CCCS

The Cleveland County Choral Society represents the pinnacle of choral excellence in North Carolina. With three decades of artistic achievement, we have established ourselves as the region's most respected voice ensemble, performing at the highest professional standards.

Our mission transcends performance—we cultivate transformative musical experiences, support emerging artists through scholarships, and enrich the cultural fabric of Cleveland County. Our meticulously curated repertoire spans classical masterworks, contemporary compositions, and spiritual arrangements performed by singers of exceptional caliber.
