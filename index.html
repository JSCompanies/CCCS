About CCCS

The Cleveland County Choral Society is North Carolina's most distinguished community choir, dedicated to enriching lives through the transformative power of choral music. Founded on principles of excellence, inclusivity, and artistic integrity, we welcome singers of all levels to join our ensemble.

Our mission extends beyond performances—we foster community connection, support local artists, and provide scholarships to emerging musicians. With a repertoire spanning classical masterworks to contemporary compositions, we deliver performances that resonate with audiences of all backgrounds.
